Best-fit use cases

Feeds and bottles

Start or save the bottle before the amount evaporates from your brain. Then use the iPhone app for fuller notes and patterns.

Diapers

Wet, dirty, mixed, and timing matter most when a pediatrician asks. Watch logging makes it less likely you miss the boring-but-important stuff.

Sleep starts and stops

Sleep logs are fragile because everyone is tired. The easier the start/stop action, the better the history.
