What to log

A good feeding tracker answers the handoff questions.

What happened?

Record bottle amount, nursing side or session note, pumping amount, formula or milk prep notes, and whether the baby finished or refused.

When did it happen?

Use timestamps for feeds, naps, diapers, and medicine so you can see the pattern instead of guessing from vibes and half-sleep math.

What changed?

Log unusual fussiness, spit-up, poor feeding, sleepy feeds, latch issues, or anything your pediatrician asked you to watch.
